Welcome to Doechii's Quotes

This application is dedicated to showcasing the words and images of the iconic artist, Doechii.

Doechii is the stage name of American rapper, singer, and songwriter Jaylah Ji'mya Hickmon. She first gained recognition on TikTok for her 2021 song "Yucky Blucky Fruitcake". Known for her energetic style, Doechii became the third woman to ever win a Grammy for Best Rap Album in February 2025. Her album beat out releases from artists like J. Cole, Eminem, and Future.

Through her artistry, Doechii continues to break barriers and redefine music.
